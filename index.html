Discover Kawasan Falls

  • About
  • Why
  • Experience Highlights

Kawasan Falls is a multi-tiered waterfall located in Badian, Cebu, just a short trip from Siquijor. Surrounded by lush greenery and crystal-clear waters, it is a serene paradise ideal for swimming and relaxation.

Get in TouchRead More

From its stunning turquoise pools to the tranquil natural environment, Kawasan Falls offers a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Whether you're cliff diving or simply soaking in the waters, Kawasan never disappoints.

Get in TouchRead More

Highlights of the Kawasan Falls experience include bamboo raft rides under the waterfalls, trekking through picturesque trails, and savoring local delicacies by the riverside.

Hot Attractions in Siquijor

Cambugahay Falls

Dive into the cool, refreshing waters of Cambugahay Falls, surrounded by lush greenery. This multi-tiered waterfall is perfect for a rejuvenating dip and scenic photos.

Learn More
Salagdoong Beach

Enjoy the sun, sand, and thrilling cliff dives at Salagdoong Beach. This beach is a favorite among both locals and tourists for its clear waters and exciting activities.

Learn More
Old Enchanted Balete Tree

Visit the mystical Old Enchanted Balete Tree, an ancient tree with a natural spring at its base. Legend has it that this tree is home to supernatural beings.

Learn More
Lazi Church and Convent

Explore the historical Lazi Church and Convent, a National Historical Landmark known for its beautiful Spanish colonial architecture.

Learn More
Paliton Beach

Paliton Beach offers stunning sunsets and a peaceful atmosphere, making it the perfect place to unwind and enjoy the natural beauty of Siquijor.

Learn More
Guiwanon Spring Park

Guiwanon Spring Park is a mangrove forest park featuring wooden pathways and serene surroundings. It's an ideal spot for nature lovers seeking tranquility.
