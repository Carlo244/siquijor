Memories for a Lifetime

  • Cambugahay Falls

    Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Cambugahay Falls, surrounded by lush greenery and the soothing sounds of nature. A perfect spot for a refreshing adventure.

  • Salagdoong Beach

    Relax on the pristine sands of Salagdoong Beach, and if you're feeling brave, take a leap off the exhilarating cliff diving spots. Sun, sea, and thrill await you.

  • Old Enchanted Balete Tree

    Uncover the mysteries of the Old Enchanted Balete Tree. This centuries-old marvel is not only a natural wonder but also has a natural spring at its baseâ€”truly enchanting.

  • Lazi Church and Convent

    Step back in time and marvel at the historical Lazi Church and Convent. A National Historical Landmark showcasing stunning Spanish colonial architecture.

  • Paliton Beach

    Experience serenity at Paliton Beach. Known for its tranquil atmosphere and picturesque sunsets, it's the perfect spot to unwind and soak in the beauty.

  • Guiwanon Spring Park

    Immerse yourself in the beauty of Guiwanon Spring Park. A mangrove forest park with wooden pathways, offering a serene escape into nature's embrace.
